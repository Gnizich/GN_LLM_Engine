these are movies i like that are considered techno thrillers

The Matrix (1999) - Keanu Reeves discovers the world is an artificial reality created by machines.
Hackers (1995) - Teen hackers get caught up in a high-tech conspiracy.
The Net (1995) - Sandra Bullock as a software engineer on the run from cyberterrorists.
Jurassic Park (1993) - Scientists clone dinosaurs using advanced bio-technology.
Minority Report (2002) - Tom Cruise as a cop in a future where crimes can be predicted before they occur.
Terminator 2: Judgment Day (1991) - Cyborg assassin from the future battles to protect a young John Connor.
WarGames (1983) - Matthew Broderick as a hacker who accidentally taps into a military simulation.
The Matrix Reloaded (2003) - Sequel continuing the exploits of Neo and crew against the machines.
Sneakers (1992) - Security experts are blackmailed into stealing a mysterious device.
TRON (1982) - Jeff Bridges gets transported into a dangerous computer program.
Eagle Eye (2008) - Shia LaBeouf and Michelle Monaghan manipulated by an artificial intelligence.
Enemy of the State (1998) - Will Smith wrongly accused of murder thanks to spy satellite technology.
Live Free or Die Hard (2007) - Bruce Willis battles cyberterrorists targeting the United States infrastructure.
