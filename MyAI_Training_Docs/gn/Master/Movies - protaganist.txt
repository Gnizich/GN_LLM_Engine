movies i enjoy watching with female protaganists

These are movies from the 1980s, 1990s, and 2000s that feature strong, capable female protagonists:

- Aliens (1986) - Sigourney Weaver stars as Ellen Ripley in this sci-fi action sequel. 

- Working Girl (1988) - Melanie Griffith plays an ambitious secretary. 

- Thelma and Louise (1991) - As mentioned previously, features Geena Davis and Susan Sarandon on a road trip gone wrong.

- G.I. Jane (1997) - Demi Moore as the first woman to undergo Navy SEAL training. 

- Mulan (1998) - Animated Disney film about a young Chinese woman who disguises herself as a man to join the army.

- Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon (2000) - Michelle Yeoh stars as a warrior in this martial arts epic. 

- Lara Croft: Tomb Raider (2001) - Angelina Jolie as the iconic adventurous archaeologist. 

- Kill Bill Vol. 1 and 2 (2003/2004) - Uma Thurman seeks vengeance in this two-part Quentin Tarantino film.

- Million Dollar Baby (2004) - Hillary Swank plays a determined boxer. 

- Mad Max: Fury Road (2015) - Charlize Theron rebels against a dictator in this acclaimed dystopian thriller.

- Star Wars: The Force Awakens (2015) - Daisy Ridley portrays the heroine Rey in this Star Wars sequel.
